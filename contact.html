Join us!

We welcome ECNU undergraduate students, especially those in the third year, to participate in our natural language processing seminars every year.

What do we like?

We like students who are diligent, motivated, good at communicating with others and like to think.
If you have time to join our lab to study together in advance, then it is best.

What do you need?

You don't need to know anything about natural language processing and machine learning. But it would be better if you know it.
You only need to have a certain time and a heart to learn. We will teach you most of the knowledge after you come here.
